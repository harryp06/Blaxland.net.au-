FAQ

The questions every
tradie asks first.

Fair questions. Here are the straight answers.

Does it act like a robot?

Absolutely not. We built Blaxland specifically to replace clunky, old, robotic phone systems. Your customers get a natural, fluid conversation with a voice that sounds real. Most callers will just assume they are speaking to your office manager.
Have a listen to our sample call at the top of the page to hear it for yourself.

How much am I charged per call?

No surprise per call fees. You pay one flat monthly price and that's it. Each plan covers to a maximum amount of calls per month (50, 100 or 130 depending on which plan you are on, which works out to be roughly 150, 300 or 400 minutes). If you're a busy outfit and need more, you just move up a plan. Always the same bill at the end of the month.

What happens with emergencies?

You tell us what counts as urgent for your trade, and Blaxland flags those calls the moment they come in, then fires off an instant SMS and email so you know straight away and can ring them back fast. Standard leads get captured and wait for you, so you're not chasing every ring.

How does call forwarding work?

There is no need to change your phone number. We use standard call forwarding to seamlessly connect your current number to your AI assistant. You can choose to have the AI answer every call instantly, or set it up as your personal backup, stepping in to catch every missed call before they go to voicemail.

What if it can't answer a question?

It won't pretend to know your quote off the top of its head. For anything it can't answer, it does the smart thing: takes the caller's name, number and what they need, and sends it straight to you so you can call back. You never lose the lead, and the customer feels looked after.

How long until it's up and running?

Most trades are live within 3 to 5 business days, and we do the whole setup for you. One personalised phone call helps us to figure out exactly what your business needs so we can create the best receptionist for you.
