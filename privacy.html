Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Harry Blaxland Parker, ABN 25 516 861 472, trading as Blaxland ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, stores and discloses your personal information. We handle personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By using our website or services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Details you give us: your name, phone number and trade when you submit our enquiry form or book a call.
  • Call information: when our AI receptionist service is active for your business, we and our providers process call audio, transcripts, caller phone numbers and the job details captured during calls, so we can pass leads to you.
  • Usage & device data: standard analytics data (pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser, referring source) collected via cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry, set up and provide the service, and contact you about it.
  • To capture and forward leads from calls to you (by SMS and email).
  • To measure and improve our website and advertising performance.

3. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run the service, including:

  • Vapi and related voice/AI providers — to power the AI receptionist and process calls.
  • Zapier — to route lead data into our systems.
  • Calendly — to schedule demo calls.
  • Google Analytics and Meta (Facebook) — for website analytics and advertising measurement.
  • Our website hosting provider.

Some of these providers may store data overseas. We take reasonable steps to ensure your information is handled securely.

4. Cookies & analytics

Our site uses cookies and pixels (Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel) to understand how visitors use the site and to measure ad performance. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, though parts of the site may not work as intended.

5. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or de-identify it.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access or correct the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing info@blaxland.net.au. If you have a complaint about how we've handled your information and you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

7. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email us at info@blaxland.net.au.
